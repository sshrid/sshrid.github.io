Hi, I'm Stephanie Shrider.

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Minnesota studying Management Information Systems and Marketing.


I am passionate about communicating technical solutions to challenging problems in a way that key stakeholders can understand and act upon. I believe that the better we become at communicating technical solutions, the better we can serve our communities.

My core values are Loyalty, Service, Honesty, Integrity, and Collaboration.

My core mission is to be a lead identifier of growth opportunities in the service industry

My core competencies include the ability to get to the root of a problem, a deep understanding of consumer behavior across cultures, a thorough understanding IT team development, high aptitude for connecting groundbreaking information, and persuasion skills.
